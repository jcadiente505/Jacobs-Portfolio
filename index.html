About Me...

Hello my name is Jacob Cadiente! I am a student at the UCF Coding Bootcamp for Full Stack Web Development. I am well versed in Front End and Back End languages and frameworks such as HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript as well as JS frameworks such as Node.js and React.js.


I originally worked in the service industry for over 6 years as a server and worked up too different manager positions such as Head Bartender of Blackstar Entertainment Venue, Bar Manager Ivanhoe Craft Bar, and Corporate Trainer Duffys Sports Grille. I studied Bio-Medical sciences at Adventist University for about 2 years as well.


To me, Web Development is the perfect blend of technical skill, logical thinking and creativity. Being a part of a team of skilled individuals working together to create a unique application I think is the best part of the job. I have worked in team settings in every job I've ever had, with coding I feel as if the work really matters.


I find creating web and mobile applications to be very rewarding work. I like that I can use my skill set to bring information too people in a fun and artistic way.
